Traffic Flow and Urban Mobility

Project Overview


This research explores the complexities of traffic flow and urban mobility, aiming to identify solutions for reducing congestion and enhancing transportation efficiency.

Traffic flow and urban mobility are critical aspects of modern city planning. Understanding the dynamics of traffic congestion and developing efficient transportation systems can significantly improve the quality of life for urban residents.
